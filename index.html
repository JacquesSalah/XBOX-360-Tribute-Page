Timeline:

2003
Initial conception and beginning of development.

November, 2005
Original release date of the "Core" and "Pro" (also simply known as "Xbox 360") models in the United States and Canada.

April and September, 2007
Release dates of the "Elite" and "Arcade" console revisions.

November, 2008
The System Software and User Interface were updated and redesigned, in an upgrade titled "New Xbox Experience"

June, 2010
Release of the "Slim" (also known as "S") console redesign, with modified hardware and a new controller version.

November, 2010
Release of the "Kinect" motion-sensing accessory and a interface update with motion features.

December, 2011
The Interface received a major overhaul in line with Microsoft's "Metro" design style.

June, 2013
Release of the "E" redesign, the last version of the Xbox 360.

November, 2013
Its successor, the Xbox One, is released.

April, 2016
Microsoft announces the end of production of the Xbox 360.
